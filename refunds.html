Refund Policy

Last updated: 2 March 2026  ·  Applies to all paid BlowUp subscriptions

We want you to love BlowUp. If something isn't right, we're here to make it fair. Below is everything you need to know about refunds on paid subscriptions.
✅
14-Day Money-Back Guarantee
If you're not satisfied within the first 14 days of your paid subscription, we'll give you a full refund — no questions asked.

1. Overview

BlowUp offers subscription-based access to our analytics platform. Subscriptions are billed in advance on a monthly or annual cycle. Because digital products provide immediate value upon activation, our refund policy is designed to be fair to both users and our team while reflecting the nature of SaaS software.

2. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

For all new paid subscriptions (Pro and Agency plans), you are entitled to a full refund if you request it within 14 calendar days of your initial payment date. This guarantee applies to:

  • First-time subscribers upgrading from the Free plan
  • Users upgrading from Pro to Agency for the first time

To claim your guarantee, contact us at billing@blowup.app within the 14-day window. No explanation required.

3. Refund Eligibility (After 14 Days)

After the 14-day guarantee period, refunds may be issued at our discretion in the following circumstances:

  • Service outage: If BlowUp experiences a verified outage lasting more than 72 consecutive hours during your billing period, you may be eligible for a pro-rated credit or refund for the affected period.
  • Billing error: If you were charged incorrectly (e.g. double-charged or charged at the wrong rate), we will issue a full refund of the incorrect charge promptly.
  • Duplicate accounts: If you accidentally created two paid accounts, we will refund one upon verification.
  • Exceptional circumstances: We review hardship requests on a case-by-case basis. Please reach out — we are reasonable people.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

The following are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Requests made after the 14-day guarantee period where no billing error occurred
  • Accounts terminated due to violation of our Terms of Service
  • Partial months of an annual subscription (except pro-rated for major service failures)
  • Forgetting to cancel before a renewal date (we recommend enabling billing reminders)
  • Dissatisfaction caused by third-party platform changes (e.g. YouTube API rate limit changes)

We strongly encourage you to use the Free plan to evaluate BlowUp before subscribing to a paid tier.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please:

  1. Email us at billing@blowup.app from the email address associated with your BlowUp account
  2. Include your full name, the date of the charge you are disputing, and the reason for your request
  3. Alternatively, use our Contact Form and select "Billing & Refunds" as the subject

Please do not initiate a chargeback with your bank before contacting us — we are almost always able to resolve issues faster and more favourably through direct communication.

6. Processing Time

  • Review: We will acknowledge your refund request within 2 business days
  • Decision: A decision will be communicated within 5 business days
  • Payment: Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card provider

Refunds are returned to the original payment method. If that method is no longer available, we will arrange an alternative.

7. Cancelling Your Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Cancellation stops future billing. Your access to paid features continues until the end of your current billing period — you are not charged for the next cycle.

Cancellation does not automatically generate a refund for the current billing period. If you believe you are owed a refund, please follow the request process above.

8. Payment Disputes

In the event of a billing dispute you are unable to resolve with us, you have the right to contact your bank or card provider. However, please note that initiating a chargeback without first contacting BlowUp may result in immediate account suspension while the dispute is investigated.

We prefer to resolve all billing issues directly and aim to do so quickly and fairly.
