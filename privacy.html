Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 March 2026  ·  Applies to all BlowUp users worldwide

Your privacy is fundamental to how we build BlowUp. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and the rights you have over your data. Please read it carefully.

1. Overview

BlowUp ("we", "us", "our") operates the BlowUp analytics platform accessible at blowup.app. We act as the data controller for personal information collected through the Service. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account Information

  • Name and email address (provided at registration)
  • Profile photo (if signed in via Google)
  • Selected subscription plan
  • Account creation and update timestamps

Social Media Connection Data

When you connect a social media account, we store:

  • YouTube: Channel ID, name, handle, subscriber count, total view count, video count, and OAuth access/refresh tokens
  • Instagram: The Instagram profile URL you provide (handle extracted client-side)
  • TikTok: The TikTok profile URL you provide (handle extracted client-side)

Usage Data

  • Pages visited, features used, and time spent on the platform
  • Browser type, device, operating system, and IP address
  • Crash reports and error logs (anonymised where possible)

Communications

  • Messages sent via our contact form
  • Support correspondence

3. How We Use Your Data

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: Providing analytics, AI recommendations, and platform features
  • Authentication: Verifying your identity and maintaining secure sessions
  • Billing: Processing subscription payments and sending invoices
  • Product improvement: Analysing usage patterns to improve features (aggregated/anonymised)
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorised access
  • Communications: Sending service updates, security alerts, and support responses
  • Legal compliance: Meeting our legal obligations and responding to lawful requests

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for unsolicited advertising or profiling unrelated to the Service.

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of BlowUp and its users.

5. Cookies & Tracking

BlowUp uses essential cookies and browser storage for:

  • Maintaining your authenticated session (Firebase Auth tokens)
  • Remembering your dashboard preferences
  • Basic analytics via Firebase Analytics (anonymised usage data)

We do not use advertising tracking pixels or sell cookie data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this will affect authentication and core functionality.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active, plus 30 days after deletion to allow recovery
  • Social media tokens: Deleted immediately when you disconnect a platform or delete your account
  • Usage/log data: Retained for up to 12 months for security and analytics purposes
  • Financial records: Retained for 7 years as required by South African tax law

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • OAuth tokens are stored in Google Cloud Firestore with strict server-side access controls
  • Firebase Security Rules ensure users can only access their own data
  • We conduct periodic security reviews of our codebase and infrastructure

Despite our best efforts, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you within the timeframes required by applicable law.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Restriction: Request that we restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is consent-based

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@blowup.app. We will respond within 30 days (or as required by law).

9. POPIA Compliance (South Africa)

BlowUp is committed to compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA). Our Information Officer is responsible for ensuring compliance. You may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa at inforegulator.org.za if you believe your rights have been violated.

10. Children's Privacy

BlowUp is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately at privacy@blowup.app and we will delete the account promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated via email or an in-app notice at least 14 days before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates the most recent revision.
